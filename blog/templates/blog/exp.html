Sentiment Analysis in Twitter

2014

  • Sentiment analysis is a mechanism for understanding the natural disposition that people possess towards a specific topic.
  • Twitter was used as main platform which is popular in Chile, with more than 5 million users in 2012.
  • The main goal was to analyse the public's opinions on the presidential primaries for the Alliance political party between two candidates and use sentiment analysis to predict the outcome of the primaries.
  • The methodology was based on a Lexicon Dictionary, using information collected during the previous months of the election and classifying each tweet as positive, negative or neutral for each candidate.
  • An exponential function was developed to extract the polarity for each candidate when both are mentioned in the tweet.
